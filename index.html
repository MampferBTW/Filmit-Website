JOHN WICK: CHAPTER 4

March 24, 2023   Chad Stahelski   Action/Neo-Noir   4.5/5★


God tier action movie


We were so excited for John Wick: Chapter 4 and it was absolutely fantastic. From the fight choreography, the soundtrack and cast to the incredible set pieces, everything was just amazing. It was hella entertaining and featured some nice twists. The atmosphere was "dark" and stylish just how you'd expect it. JW 4 features a lot of sequences that you won't forget so easily.
